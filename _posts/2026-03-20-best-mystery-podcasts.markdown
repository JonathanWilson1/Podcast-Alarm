<p>The best mystery podcasts keep you guessing right up to the end — and sometimes beyond it. Whether you're drawn to cold cases, unsolved disappearances or real detective work, these 10 shows are the ones worth subscribing to.</p>

{% include callToActionRow.html %}

<br>

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=Casefile+True+Crime">1. Casefile True Crime</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-mystery-podcasts/casefile-true-crime.jpg" alt="Casefile True Crime podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>The gold standard for mystery and true crime. An anonymous Australian host narrates meticulously researched cases — missing persons, cold cases, wrongful convictions — with zero sensationalism. If you only listen to one show on this list, make it Casefile.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=Up+and+Vanished">2. Up and Vanished</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-mystery-podcasts/up-and-vanished.jpg" alt="Up and Vanished podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>Payne Lindsey started this as an amateur investigation into the disappearance of Georgia teacher Tara Grinstead — and ended up helping solve it. One of the few true crime podcasts that can say it directly contributed to an arrest.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=Someone+Knows+Something+CBC">3. Someone Knows Something</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-mystery-podcasts/someone-knows-something.jpg" alt="Someone Knows Something podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>CBC's David Ridgen investigates cold cases alongside families still searching for answers. The show has actually helped solve cases. That alone makes it unlike anything else in the genre.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=Your+Own+Backyard+podcast">4. Your Own Backyard</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-mystery-podcasts/your-own-backyard.jpg" alt="Your Own Backyard podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>Chris Lambert's long-running investigation into the disappearance of Kristin Smart from Cal Poly in 1996. Obsessively detailed, community-driven, and ultimately led to a conviction. A masterclass in citizen investigation.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=Bear+Brook+podcast">5. Bear Brook</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-mystery-podcasts/bear-brook.jpg" alt="Bear Brook podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>New Hampshire Public Radio's investigation into four unidentified murder victims found in barrels in the New Hampshire woods — a case that eventually cracked open thanks to DNA genealogy. Gripping and genuinely groundbreaking.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=Swindled+podcast">6. Swindled</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-mystery-podcasts/swindled.jpg" alt="Swindled podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>A slightly different kind of mystery — corporate crime, regulatory failures and white-collar wrongdoing, narrated by an anonymous host with a very dry wit. Funny, infuriating and endlessly listenable.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=Dirty+John+podcast">7. Dirty John</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-mystery-podcasts/dirty-john.jpg" alt="Dirty John podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>The LA Times investigation into con man John Meehan and the woman he deceived. Told in gripping serial form, it covers manipulation, obsession and a family pushed to breaking point. The TV adaptation followed — the podcast came first.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=Over+My+Dead+Body+Wondery">8. Over My Dead Body</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-mystery-podcasts/over-my-dead-body.jpg" alt="Over My Dead Body podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>Wondery's narrative true crime series covers infamous cases with dramatic flair — but crucially, it gets the facts right. Each season covers a different story and works brilliantly as a binge listen.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=Scam+Goddess+podcast">9. Scam Goddess</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-mystery-podcasts/scam-goddess.jpg" alt="Scam Goddess podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>Comedian Laci Mosley breaks down history's greatest scams and con artists with her comedian guests. More fun than it has any right to be — and you'll learn exactly how people get fooled.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=Darknet+Diaries">10. Darknet Diaries</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-mystery-podcasts/darknet-diaries.jpg" alt="Darknet Diaries podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>Jack Rhysider investigates hacks, breaches, cybercrime and the hidden parts of the internet. Each episode is a self-contained mystery — the kind where the twist is always that someone was much cleverer (or stupider) than you expected.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">
