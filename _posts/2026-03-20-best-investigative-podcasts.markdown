<p>Investigative podcasts are journalism at its best — reporters spending months or years on a single story, uncovering things powerful people would rather stay buried. These 10 shows have broken news, changed laws and freed innocent people.</p>

{% include callToActionRow.html %}

<br>

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=Serial+podcast">1. Serial</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-investigative-podcasts/serial.jpg" alt="Serial podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>The show that proved podcasts could do serious journalism. Sarah Koenig's investigation into the 1999 murder of Hae Min Lee — and whether Adnan Syed was wrongly convicted — spawned a genre. Still essential listening.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=Radiolab+WNYC">2. Radiolab</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-investigative-podcasts/radiolab.jpg" alt="Radiolab podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>WNYC's long-running science and philosophy show is more investigative than it appears — every episode starts with a question and follows the evidence wherever it goes. Jad Abumrad and Robert Krulwich built a template that a hundred podcasts have tried to copy.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=Reveal+investigative+reporting">3. Reveal</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-investigative-podcasts/reveal.jpg" alt="Reveal podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>From the Center for Investigative Reporting, Reveal covers everything from corporate wrongdoing to government failure to environmental crime. Consistently rigorous, consistently important.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=Planet+Money+NPR">4. Planet Money</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-investigative-podcasts/planet-money.jpg" alt="Planet Money podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>NPR's economics team regularly breaks stories buried in spreadsheets and regulatory filings — making them entertaining in the process. More investigative than it looks on the surface.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=Drilled+podcast+Amy+Westervelt">5. Drilled</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-investigative-podcasts/drilled.jpg" alt="Drilled podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>Amy Westervelt's investigation into the fossil fuel industry's decades-long campaign to confuse the public on climate change. Painstakingly sourced, genuinely alarming, and brilliantly produced.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=Dirty+John+Wondery">6. Dirty John</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-investigative-podcasts/dirty-john.jpg" alt="Dirty John podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>The LA Times investigation into con man John Meehan — painstakingly reported, compulsively listenable. One of the first examples of a newspaper breaking a major story in podcast form rather than print.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=The+Dropout+Elizabeth+Holmes">7. The Dropout</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-investigative-podcasts/the-dropout.jpg" alt="The Dropout podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>ABC Audio's investigation into Elizabeth Holmes and the Theranos fraud — told with remarkable access and detail. If you want to understand how a charismatic founder can deceive investors, journalists and patients simultaneously, start here.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=This+American+Life">8. This American Life</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-investigative-podcasts/this-american-life.jpg" alt="This American Life podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>Ira Glass's weekly show has been doing investigative storytelling since 1995. The team behind Serial came from here. Several episodes have directly sparked legal investigations and policy changes — a rare claim for any media outlet.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=Freakonomics+Radio">9. Freakonomics Radio</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-investigative-podcasts/freakonomics-radio.jpg" alt="Freakonomics Radio podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>Stephen Dubner applies the tools of investigative journalism to unexpected questions — why do drug dealers live with their mothers, what really reduces crime, who cheats and why. Rigorous sourcing, counterintuitive conclusions, endlessly listenable.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">

<h3><a class="text-info" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/gb/search?term=Rough+Translation+NPR">10. Rough Translation</a></h3>
<img src="/images/blog/charts/best-investigative-podcasts/rough-translation.jpg" alt="Rough Translation podcast artwork" width="100" style="border-radius:12px;float:left;margin:0 15px 15px 0;">
<p>NPR's foreign correspondents investigate stories from outside the US — often the same themes Americans are wrestling with, playing out differently abroad. Quietly one of the best shows on this list.</p>
<br style="clear:both;">
